Transport Facility
The academy has arranged its own college buses for safe commuting of students from their places to college. It fully ensures to save student’s precious time. The bus facility is available for students of Islampur City and nearby areas.
Hostel and Mess Facility
The Rajarambapu Paramount Career Academy provides a comfortable accommodation for its students. It’s a second home which ensures that the students live their college life in a healthy and safe environment
  • Dedicated Girls and Boys Hostel Facility
  • CCTV cameras at entrances of all hostels and foyer inside the campus
  • 24x7 electricity and water supply in all the hostels
  • Solar panels installed in all hostels for hot water
  • Caretakers and housekeeping services are provided in all hostels during day and night
  • RO water used for cooking, mess is well maintained and care is taken to ensure hygiene.
Study Material
In collaboration with experienced faculties, The Research and Development wing of our institute undertakes the task of preparation of course modules for JEE, NEET, AIIMS and NDA. The study modules comprise of - a) Concept Booster, B) Chapter at a glance, C) Miscellaneous Questions, D) Spark Questions, E) Entrance exam zone, F) Solved and unsolved questions, G) Question papers of Previous Exams.. Study material covers short notes, figures, diagrams, formulae, equation, HOT questions, etc.
Smart Classroom
The spacious classrooms on campus are equipped with LCD projectors to implement an interactive learning experience for the students. The design of the classrooms facilitates a free exchange of ideas between students and teachers. The smart board saves teachers time while drawing graphs and logical diagrams. The smart projector allows the teachers to take their teaching out of class and connect it to wider world of internet allowing them to use video files, PPT and other online resources as per their choice. The campus also has Conference and Seminar halls with audio visual aids and multimedia facilities.
Night Study Facility
RPA stands out different among others because of its “Night Supervised Study”. Teachers are available during night study for clearing the doubts.
Computer Labs
The computer lab with Wi-Fi and the Internet connectivity is an integral part of our system. Students gain latest knowledge and IT skills using the computer labs.
Self-Assessment Programme
SAP is self-assessment programme. Students are assigned questions to solve as per the NEET/JEE Pattern on daily basis in a scheduled class. One subjects SAP activity is conducted every day. Discussion class is conducted after the student submit their SAP assignment. It gives them opportunity to rectify their mistakes immediately.
