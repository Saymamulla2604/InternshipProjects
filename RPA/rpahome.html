What We Offer?

JEE
Aspiring students in India, passing the JEE (Joint Entrance Exam) is the ticket to getting admission in some of the prestigious and best engineering colleges in the country, particularly the NITs, IITs, IISC, and state-funded technical institutes.
NEET
Do you want to change your dream into reality? Do you want to be a doctor? Then you should prepare yourself for NEET. You can research the courses after qualifying for the NEET exam. NEET is the All India Entrance Test for students who want to be doctors in the future.
NDA
Serving the nation is one of the biggest reasons to join the NDA, and so is a good career option. Candidates who join the Indian Army provide their country with selfless service and give them a sense of pride
MH-CET
The Common Entrance Test (CET) is a competitive exam conducted for the purpose of admission of students to the first year or first semester of full-time courses in medical, dental and engineering courses in professional colleges the various states of India.
Why Choose Us?
Integrated Course with Boards
We provide not only courses like Mains & Advanced JEE, NEET and NDA but also we offer the training of CET, 11th Board, 12th Board and also Foundation of 8,9,10th .We provide student training for SSB classes. We develop skills like personality development program, English communication.
Transport Facility
The academy has arranged its own college buses for safe commuting of students from their places to college. It fully ensures to save student’s precious time. The bus facility is available for students of Islampur City and nearby areas.
Hostel and Mess Facility
Our hostel has following facilities:- Dedicated Girls and Boys Hostel Facility, Campus under CCTV surveillance, 24x7 electricity and water supply, Solar panels for hot water, Caretakers and housekeeping services 24x7, RO water used for cooking, mess is well maintained and care is taken to ensure hygiene.
Study Material
We provide study material for our students for the courses like JEE, NEET, AIIMS and NDA. The material consists of Concept Booster, Chapter at a glance, Miscellaneous Questions, Spark Questions, Entrance exam zone, Solved and unsolved questions, Question papers of previous exams.
Smart Classroom
The classrooms are equipped with LCD projector. The smart board saves teachers time while drawing graphs and logical diagrams. The smart projector allows the teachers to take their teaching out of class and connect it to wider world of internet allowing them to use video files, PPT and other online resources as per their choice. The campus also has Conference and Seminar halls with audio visual aids and multimedia facilities.
Night Study Facility
RPA stands out different among others because of its “Night Supervised Study”. Teachers are available during night study for clearing the doubts.
Computer Labs
The computer lab with Wi-Fi and the Internet connectivity is an integral part of our system. Students gain latest knowledge and IT skills using the computer labs.
Self-Assessment Programme
SAP is self-assessment programme. Students are assigned questions to solve as per the NEET/JEE Pattern on daily basis in a scheduled class. One subjects SAP activity is conducted every day. Discussion class is conducted after the student submit their SAP assignment. It gives them opportunity to rectify their mistakes immediately.
