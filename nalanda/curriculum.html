Rotation of LEVELS at NALANDA

The curriculum of each module is divided in Levels; each module has 1 to 6 well designed Levels. Each day all six levels of the three modules body, soul and brain run, this will cater to need of slow and fast learners.

Every level gets 64 hours of effective teaching and learning and our well designed curriculum will be effectively delivered along with scope for the learner to transit between two levels.

How it works

We at NALANDA have come up with a structure that will cater to the time needs of every learner, by introducing Levels, each level will run everyday all year round. The learner can achieve the level according to his/her own pace and understanding effectively without any time barrier. The level concept is explained in depth during personal Counselling sessions.

The other important aspect we at NALANDA believe in is that, most of the development of an individual in terms of Brain takes place in the age of 0 to 6 years, this is a well recorded fact not only in current times, but has been passed on through generations. We wish to introduce our unique and holistic Education System from the same pivotal age group of 0 to 6 years.
